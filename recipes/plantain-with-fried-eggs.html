Boiled Plantain With (Nigerian Style) Fried Eggs.

Description

She's iconic, she doesn't waste time and can be brought in at any time within a 24 hour period.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 3 plantain
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 Fresh peppers
  • 5 big Fresh tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Cut the plantain and boil add salt, boil for 12 minutes and drain the water.
  2. Break the egg in a bowl and beat till is foamy.
  3. Add salt.
  4. Slice the fresh pepper,fresh tomatoes and onion.
  5. Heat the vegetable up in a frying pan and pour in the onion.
  6. Fry for 20 seconds,pour in the fresh tomatoes and fresh pepper fry for 5 minutes add salt.
  7. Stir very well.
  8. Pour in the whisked egg and fry for 2 minutes then stir and bring down from the heat.
Go back to home.
